About NCVET

National Council for Vocational Education and Training

As the national regulator for India's vocational education and training ecosystem, NCVET is committed to ensuring quality and standardization in skill development across the country. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing robust frameworks that align with industry needs.

The AI-powered guidance system is part of our initiative to democratize access to quality vocational training and ensure that every learner can navigate their unique pathway to success in alignment with the National Skills Qualifications Framework (NSQF).
